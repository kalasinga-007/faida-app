Terms of Service

1. Data Storage
All your business data (products, sales, expenses, debts, customers, workers, M-Pesa transactions) is stored locally on your device. Faida does not upload or store your business data on our servers.

2. Free Trial & Subscription
You get a 30-day free trial with full access. After 30 days, KES 199/month is required for unlimited products. Your first 10 products are always free — no expiry.

3. User Responsibilities
You are responsible for the accuracy of data you enter. Faida is a management tool — always verify totals against your physical records and M-Pesa statements.

4. Acceptable Use
Faida is for legitimate business management only. You agree not to use it for fraudulent, illegal, or harmful purposes.

5. Service Updates
We may update features over time. We will notify you of major changes via the app. Continued use after changes means you accept the updated terms.

Privacy Policy

What We Collect
During registration: your name, phone number, email, and business name. This is stored in your browser's local storage — on your device only.

Your Business Data
Products, sales, expenses, debts, customer lists, worker records, and M-Pesa transactions are stored only on your device. We do not access, read, or transmit this data to any server.

Data Security
Your data is as secure as your device. We strongly recommend setting a PIN lock within the app (Settings → Change PIN) for additional security.

Data Sharing
We do not sell, share, or rent your personal data to any third parties. Period. Your business information is yours alone.

Your Rights
You can export all your data at any time from Settings → Export My Data. You can delete all data from Settings → Clear All Data. You can delete your entire account from Settings → Delete Account.
